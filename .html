Kit Kat: Have a Break, Have a Kit Kat!

Kit Kat is a delicious chocolate-covered wafer bar that has been a fan favorite since 1935!

Fun Facts About Kit Kat

  • Kit Kat was originally created in the UK.
  • Japan has over 300 unique Kit Kat flavors!
  • It was named after the Kit-Cat Club in London.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 218 per 4 fingers | Sugar: 21g | Fat: 11g
